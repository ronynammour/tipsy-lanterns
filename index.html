Tipsy Lanterns

Something About Us:

Tipsy lanterns was established in 2010, is an immersive Asian-inspired dining experience where tradition meets modern flair. Established with the vision of blending authentic flavors with a vibrant, electrifying atmosphere, the restaurant takes you on a sensory journey like no other.

Bask under the glow of colorful neon lights, reminiscent of bustling cityscapes in Tokyo or Hong Kong, while enjoying a menu curated with bold and innovative Asian fusion dishes. From tantalizing street-style snacks to exquisite signature cocktails, every element is crafted to ignite your taste buds and elevate your evening.

Whether you're dining with friends, celebrating milestones, or simply seeking an unforgettable night out, Tipsy Lanterns promises to leave you enchanted with its unique fusion of culture, cuisine, and cutting-edge ambiance. Step into the glow and let the lanterns guide your way to a night full of flavor and excitement!
